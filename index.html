Banana Bread with a Twist

A yummy new take on classic banana bread

by thesapor on Tik Tok

Total Cook Time: Approximately 2 hours

Ingredients

For the bread you will need:

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

For the streusel topping you will need:

  • 2/3 cup all purpose flower
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 5 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons cinnamon

Directions

  1. To make your topping n a bowl combine flower, brown sugar, salte, and stir in mixture until crumbly

    Time 5-6 minutes

  2. To make your bread mix together your mashed bananas, melted butter, granulated + brown sugar, eggs, sour cream, vanilla extract, and salt. Once all the ingridients are thoroughly mixed together and a smooth consistency pour the batter into a 1.5 pound loaf pan and top with the stresual mixture.

    Time 20-30 minutes

  3. Ensure your oven is preheated and begin cooking, covering bread with foil if the top begins to brown to quickly

    Time 55-70 minutes
