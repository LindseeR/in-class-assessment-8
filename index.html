Banana Bread with a Twist

A yummy new take on classic banana bread

by thesapor on Tik Tok

Total Cook Time: Approximately 2 hours

Ingredients

For the bread you will need:

    3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 1/4 cup all purpose flower
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking sodea
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

For the streusel topping you will need:

  • 2/3 cup all purpose flower
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 5 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons cinnamon

Directions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F
  2. combine flour, brown sugar, cinnamon, and salt. Stir in melted butter until the mixture is crumbly.
  3. mix together the mashed bananas, melted butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and eggs. Stir in the sour cream, vanilla extract, flour, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Pour batter into 1.5 pound loaf pan and top with stresusel mixture
  5. Bake at 350 for an hour to hour and a half
  6. Let cool for 15 to 20 minutes
